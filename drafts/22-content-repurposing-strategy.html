Content Repurposing: Turn 1 Video Into 10 Pieces of Content

Published: May 28, 2026

Top creators don't create more — they repurpose smarter. One YouTube video can fuel an entire week of content.

Start With Long-Form (YouTube)

Your 10-20 minute YouTube video is the content pillar. Everything else derives from it.

Clip 3-5 Shorts/Reels/TikToks

Extract the most punchy, standalone moments. Add captions and post across platforms.

Write a LinkedIn Post

Summarize the core insight in text format. LinkedIn rewards original text posts with organic reach.

Create a Twitter/X Thread

Break the video into 8-10 tweet-sized insights. End with a link to the full video.

Write a Newsletter Summary

Send a 300-word summary to your email list. Email converts 40x better than social media.

Turn Key Points Into Instagram Carousels

Educational carousels consistently get saved and shared — the highest-value Instagram engagement metric.
