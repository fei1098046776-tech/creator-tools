5 CapCut Editing Tricks for Higher Retention

Category: Editing • April 2026

The J-Cut

A J-Cut is when the audio of the next clip starts playing before the video cuts. This subconsciously pulls the viewer into the next scene, drastically reducing swipe-aways.

Dynamic Captions

Static text is dead. Use CapCut's auto-captions and apply the 'Spring' or 'Bounce' animation to make one word pop up at a time, keeping the viewer's eyes glued to the center of the screen.

Sound Design is 50% of the Video

Add a low 'whoosh' sound effect to every text pop-up and zoom transition. Good audio design makes a video feel professional and satisfying to watch.
