TikTok SEO: How to Rank Your Videos in Search

Published: [DATE]

TikTok is increasingly used as a search engine, especially by Gen Z. By optimizing your videos for TikTok SEO, your content can gain passive views months after publishing. Here is how to rank your videos.

1. Keyword Research

Type your niche topic into the TikTok search bar. Look at the auto-suggested terms below it — these are search queries users are typing in. Choose 1-2 keywords for your video title.

2. Speak Your Keywords Aloud

TikTok's AI transcribes your audio to understand what your video is about. Say your primary keywords in the first 3-5 seconds of your video.

3. Use In-Video Text Overlays

Add your main keyword in text overlays on the screen. The algorithm reads on-screen text to categorize videos.

4. Optimize the Caption and Hashtags

Write a detailed caption (up to 150-200 words) using your keywords naturally. Include 3-4 highly relevant hashtags (e.g., #VideoEditingTip rather than generic tags like #FYP).
