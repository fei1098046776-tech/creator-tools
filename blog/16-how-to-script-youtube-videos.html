How to Script YouTube Videos That Keep Viewers Watching

Published: May 16, 2026

Most creators wing it and wonder why viewers drop off. A solid script is the foundation of every high-retention video.

The Hook (First 30 Seconds)

Tell viewers exactly what they'll get and why they should care. Avoid long intros — start with the value immediately.

The Promise

State what you'll cover: 'By the end of this video, you'll know exactly how to...' This sets expectations and keeps people watching.

The Body — Use the PAS Formula

  • Problem: Identify the pain point your viewer has
  • Agitate: Make them feel the problem
  • Solution: Deliver your answer clearly

The Loop Technique

Tease upcoming content throughout the video: 'I'll share the most important tip at the end...' This reduces drop-off.

The CTA

One clear call to action. Don't ask viewers to subscribe AND like AND comment. Pick one.
