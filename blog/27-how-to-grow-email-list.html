How to Build and Grow an Email List for Content Creators

Published: [DATE]

Social media platforms can change their algorithm or ban accounts overnight, wiping out years of hard work. An email list is the only marketing channel you truly own. Here is how to build yours starting today.

1. Create a Lead Magnet

People rarely sign up for a newsletter just for updates. Give them a valuable free resource in exchange for their email: a Notion template, a PDF checklist, or a mini-course.

2. Choose the Right Platform

For content creators, **ConvertKit (Kit)**, **Beehiiv**, and **MailerLite** are the top platforms. They offer free plans for your first 1,000 subscribers and include easy landing page builders.

3. Promote Everywhere

Place your opt-in link in your YouTube descriptions, your TikTok/Instagram bio, and at the end of every blog post. Mention your free guide naturally inside your videos.

4. Send Consistent Value

Write to your list weekly. Share one actionable tip, one tool review, and link to your latest video. Keep it personal and conversational.
