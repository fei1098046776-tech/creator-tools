YouTube Shorts Monetization Explained

Category: Monetization • April 2026

The 10 Million Requirement

To enter the YouTube Partner Program via Shorts, you need 1,000 subscribers and 10 million valid Shorts views in the last 90 days. It sounds daunting, but one viral Short can cross that threshold instantly.

How the Revenue Pool Works

Unlike long-form videos where you keep 55% of the ad revenue, Shorts revenue is pooled globally and distributed based on your share of total views, minus music licensing costs.

Maximizing Your RPM

Finance, software, and real estate Shorts have significantly higher RPMs (Revenue Per Mille) than gaming or meme compilation Shorts. Choose your niche wisely.
