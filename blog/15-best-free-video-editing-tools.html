Best Free Video Editing Tools for Creators in 2026

Published: May 14, 2026

You don't need expensive software to create professional videos. These free tools deliver outstanding results.

DaVinci Resolve

The most powerful free video editor available. Used by Hollywood professionals. Includes color grading, audio tools, and effects — all free.

CapCut (Mobile + Desktop)

Best for short-form content. Auto-captions, trending effects, and direct TikTok/Reels export. Ideal for beginners.

kdenlive

Open-source and fully featured. Great for Linux users or anyone who wants complete control without subscriptions.

OpenShot

Simple, beginner-friendly, and available on all platforms. Best for basic cuts and simple edits.

Which Should You Choose?

Start with CapCut if you're creating short-form content. Graduate to DaVinci Resolve when you need professional control.
