How to Repurpose Long-Form Videos into Shorts and TikToks

Published: [DATE]

Creating content from scratch for every platform is exhausting. Smart creators use a repurposing strategy. One 10-minute YouTube video can fuel your TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts for a week. Here is how.

1. Find the Climax Moments

Look at your YouTube audience retention graph. The spikes show where viewers were most engaged. These 30-60 second segments are your best candidates for short-form clips.

2. Edit for Vertical Formats

Crop the horizontal video to a 9:16 aspect ratio. Keep your face centered. If you are showing a screen share, place your talking head at the top and the screen capture at the bottom.

3. Use Automated AI Clipping Tools

If you want to save time, tools like **Opus Clip**, **Munch**, and **Vidiofy** use AI to find engaging moments, crop them vertically, and generate stylized auto-captions automatically.

4. Add Fast Hooks

Short-form viewers have short attention spans. Edit out any pauses, breaths, or filler words. Use zoom-ins and sound effects every few seconds to hold attention.
