About CreatorCalc

The creator economy is booming, but it's also incredibly opaque. We built CreatorCalc because we realized that thousands of talented creators—from YouTubers to TikTokers and Podcasters—were massively undercharging for their work or had no idea what their metrics actually meant.

Our mission is simple: To bring transparency and real data to content creators everywhere.

We provide 100% free, fast, and secure calculators that do not require you to sign in or hand over your social media passwords. We leverage aggregated industry data, real brand deal insights, and advanced algorithms to give you the most accurate estimations possible.
