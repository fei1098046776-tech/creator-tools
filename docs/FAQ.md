# FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### Who is creator-tools for?
creator-tools is designed for **independent content creators** — especially those who are non-technical and operate across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Shopify, and podcast platforms. You don't need to know how to code to use most of the tools.

### Is this free to use?
Yes, 100% free and open-source under the MIT License.

### Do I need to install anything?
No. Most tools are static HTML pages — just open them in any browser. No installation, no account, no paywall.

---

## Tools

### How does the YouTube Money Calculator work?
Enter your estimated monthly views and your niche's average CPM (cost per thousand views). The calculator gives you a realistic revenue estimate based on these inputs.

### How accurate are the income estimates?
They are estimates based on industry averages. Actual earnings vary significantly by niche, audience location, and monetization method.

### Can I use these tools on mobile?
Yes, all tools are mobile-responsive.

## AI Features (Coming Soon)

### When will the AI features launch?
We're actively developing Phase 1 AI features (script analyzer and metadata generator). Follow this repo for updates, or watch the [Roadmap](./ROADMAP.md).

### What AI model will power the features?
We plan to use OpenAI's API for text analysis and generation. All AI features will remain free and open-source.

### Will my scripts or content be stored?
No. When AI features launch, inputs will be processed in real-time and not stored.

## Contributing

### How can I contribute?
See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for full details. In short: fork the repo, make your changes, and open a pull request.

### I'm not a developer — can I still help?
Absolutely. You can help by:
- Opening issues with feature requests or bugs
- Improving documentation
- Sharing the project with other creators
- Giving feedback on existing tools
