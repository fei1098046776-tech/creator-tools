# Quick Start — For Non-Technical Creators

No coding needed. This guide is for creators who want to use creator-tools right away.

---

## Step 1: Open a Tool

All tools are simple web pages. Just click the link and open in your browser:

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| [YouTube Money Calculator](./youtube-money-calculator.html) | Estimate your YouTube ad revenue |
| [Instagram Engagement Calculator](./instagram-engagement-calculator.html) | Check your real engagement rate |
| [TikTok Engagement Calculator](./tiktok-engagement-calculator.html) | Measure TikTok performance |
| [Podcast Growth Calculator](./podcast-growth-calculator.html) | Project your audience growth |
| [Sponsorship Rate Calculator](./sponsorship-rate-calculator.html) | Know your fair sponsorship price |
| [Content Creator Income Estimator](./content-creator-income-estimator.html) | Multi-platform income overview |

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Numbers

Each tool has labeled input fields. Fill in the numbers you know — estimates are fine.

**Example (YouTube Money Calculator):**
- Monthly views: `50,000`
- Your niche CPM: `$3.50` (Gaming = ~$2, Finance = ~$10, check Social Blade for your niche)

---

## Step 3: Read Your Results

Results appear instantly. No account needed. No data is saved.
